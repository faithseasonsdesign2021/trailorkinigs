    • Curtainsiders


    Some Key Functions of our Curtainsiders


    • Flush fitting doors with integral locks optimising space for up to 34 pallets and giving a clean face for sign writting.
    • Extruded Aluminium cant rail unique to TRAILORKINGS pre stressed and designed to maintain a correct size aperture and create minimum friction and maximum ease of operation of curtains, post and sliding roof.
    • Side mounted full length sliding posts specially manufactured to eliminate risk to the operator during opening and closing.
    • Flush one-piece front GRP bulkhead with wrap-around corners increasing curtain protection and allowing for secure pole location with maximising weather protection.
    • Rolled heavy duty flush steel rear pillars providing maximum hinge protection minimising damange.
    • Bolt-on aluminium side guards with fixed or folding options.
    • Interlocked hardwoods floor fitted as standard with options of a variety of aluminium and steels in-lays and overlays.
    • 2 x 2 weave curtain ensuring a secure quality load restraint and quality finish.
    • Load restraint options : center or side roof mounted, other options include spider trap systems to suit a variety of loads including roll cages
    • Second deck options : fixed, center swivel, butterfly or the versetile ratchet.
    • Buckleless Tensioning system with manual or air operation
